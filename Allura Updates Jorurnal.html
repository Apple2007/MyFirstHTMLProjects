What is Allura Updates?

By: Jack Shorenstein

The Whole Thing

December 20, 2018 - January 6, 2019

It all started when I watched the last episode of Voltron: Legandary Defender. I saw with my own eyes that Lance got Alltan marks by Allura kissing Lance. I really wanted to know why this happend. So, I went on the internet to find out. All I found were people talking about it and making guesses about why he got the marks. After a lot of work, I got nowhere. So, I stopped doing it and stopped looking. This was the story of Allura Updates.
